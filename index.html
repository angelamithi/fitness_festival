Disability & Special Needs

SUPPORT PROGRAMME

Fitness Festival 2026 operates a dedicated Disability and Special Needs Support Zone, ensuring that persons with disabilities and children with special needs are not merely included as spectators but are active, celebrated participants in the event. This zone will deliver four flagship free services to the community.

👂
✓ FREE Service

Hearing Aids

  • Free hearing aids for children & adults with hearing impairments
  • On-site assessments by qualified audiologists
  • Pre-registration via schools, community health workers & hospitals
🦯
✓ FREE Service

White Canes

  • Free white canes for persons with visual impairments
  • Essential for safe, independent navigation in rural areas
  • Distribution via Kenya Union of the Blind & local health units
♿
✓ FREE Service

Wheelchairs

  • Free wheelchairs for adults & children with mobility challenges
  • Beneficiaries pre-screened via hospitals, schools & health workers
  • On-site fitting, mobility training & follow-up physiotherapy referrals
🩺
✓ FREE Service

Physiotherapy Services

  • Dedicated clinic staffed by 2+ licensed physiotherapists
  • Musculoskeletal assessments, mobility & gait training, pain management
  • Special focus: cerebral palsy, developmental delays & post-surgical conditions
  • Written referral letters & home exercise guidance for caregivers

🎯 Target Beneficiaries for Assistive Devices & Physiotherapy

👂 Children with hearing impairments 20–30
🦯 Persons with visual impairments 20–30
♿ Persons with mobility challenges 10–20
🩺 Physiotherapy beneficiaries 50–100
Pre-registration for assistive device distribution will open from June 1, 2026. Community health workers, schools for children with special needs, hospitals, and disability organisations will be engaged to identify and refer eligible beneficiaries in advance of the event.

📍 Geographic Focus

  • Kapsabet town and surrounding sub-counties
  • Tea-growing communities throughout Nandi County
  • All Nandi County residents seeking health status assessment through fitness
  • Neighbouring counties interested in wellness participation
